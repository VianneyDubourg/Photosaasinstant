## Design notes

**Layout rules:**
- Every slide has a consistent dark background (`#05050a` or `#0a0a14`)
- Purple glow/gradient accents on key slides (cover, CTA, key stats)
- White is for headlines and important text
- `rgba(255,255,255,0.5-0.6)` for body/secondary text
- Purple `#a78bfa` for highlighted words, stats, accent elements
- Green only for "Budget booking" option
- Consistent padding: generous whitespace, never crowded

**Typography hierarchy:**
- Slide titles: very large, bold, white
- Sub-titles: medium, light purple or white/70
- Body text: small-medium, white/60
- Stats/numbers: huge, bold, purple or white
- Labels/tags: tiny, uppercase, letter-spaced

**Recurring elements:**
- `vlogo.fr` small bottom-right on every slide
- Slide number bottom-left (optional)
- Thin purple top border line on each slide (like a header underline)

**Export format:** PDF, 16:9 (1920×1080px or A4 landscape equivalent)
